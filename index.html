Technical Manual

Overview

This Marriage Matching Module performs comprehensive compatibility analysis using both BPHS (Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra) and KP (Krishnamurti Paddhati) systems of Vedic Astrology.

Calculation Methods

Planetary Positions

Planetary longitudes are calculated using simplified astronomical algorithms based on orbital elements. The calculations account for mean anomaly, equation of center, and perturbation effects for each planet.

Ayanamsa

The Lahiri (Chitrapaksha) Ayanamsa is used to convert tropical positions to sidereal. The ayanamsa value is computed based on the precession of equinoxes from the epoch.

House Cusps

House cusps are calculated using the Placidus system, which divides the diurnal and nocturnal arcs into equal time intervals.

Assessment Systems

BPHS Assessment

Based on Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, this evaluates house lordships, planetary significations, yogas, and dasha compatibility between the two charts.

KP Assessment

The Krishnamurti Paddhati system analyzes sub-lord positions and their significations to determine marriage prospects and compatibility.

Ashtakoota (8 Koota)

Traditional 8-point matching system evaluating Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakoot, and Nadi with a maximum score of 36 points.

Dashakoota (10 Koota)

Extended 10-point matching system that adds Mahendra and Vedha to the standard 8 kootas for more comprehensive assessment.

Marriage Timeline

The timeline is derived by analyzing Vimshottari Dasha periods of the 7th house lord, Venus (karaka for marriage), and relevant sub-periods that activate marriage-signifying houses (2, 7, 11).

20-Year Forecast

The forecast analyzes Dasha, Antardasha, and Pratyantardasha periods along with major planetary transits over Saturn, Jupiter, and Rahu-Ketu cycles to project relationship strengths and challenges.

Health Compatibility

Health assessment examines the 6th and 8th house significations, planetary afflictions, and shared vulnerabilities in both charts to evaluate physical and mental health compatibility.
